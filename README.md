<h1 align="center">Ekko</h1>
<p align="center"><i>Say it like no one is listening.</i></p>

Ekko is a privacy platform: one private identity for every app you already use.
Messages seal themselves on-device with hybrid post-quantum encryption
(X25519 + ML-KEM-768) and travel through the apps you already have open, no
new app for your friends to install.

**Status: private alpha.** A small group is testing the extension right now.
This repo is the future home of the open-source release.

## What's coming

- **Open source.** The extension, protocol, and directory server, published here.
- **Self-hosting.** Run your own directory off the grid, no dependency on us.
- **Reproducible builds.** Compile your own binaries and verify what's running.
- **Keyboard extension.** A phone-side keyboard so encryption travels with you, not just the browser.
